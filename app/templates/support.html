Frequently Asked Questions

How do I crop an image?

Click the crop tool icon, drag to select the area you want to keep, and click "Apply" to crop your image.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats. Files must be under 16MB.

What is the maximum supported resolution?

We support images upto 2000 * 2000 pixels .
